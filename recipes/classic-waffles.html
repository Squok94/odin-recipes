Classic Waffles

Description

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups warm milk
  • Half a cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
